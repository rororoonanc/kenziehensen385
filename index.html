About Kenzie

A versatile talent with a passion for storytelling across multiple mediums

Mckenzie Peterson

Known professionally as Kenzie Hensen

Mckenzie Peterson, known professionally as Kenzie Hensen, is a highly accomplished and versatile Professional Model & Actress who works in multiple other fields in the Entertainment Industry namely in the Production side. Kenzie has won Multiple Acting Awards at film festivals.

She was born January of 2000 in Fort Bragg North Carolina spending most of her childhood traveling the world. Currently, Kenzie spends her days traveling the country on different Movie & TV sets and Runway Productions. She is Currently signed to two (4) different Talent Agencies and one (2) Management Group.
